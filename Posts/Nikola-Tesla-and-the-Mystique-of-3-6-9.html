Nikola Tesla and the Mystique of 3-6-9

Unraveling the Enigma

Posted by Gülşah Tosun on October 8, 2023 Edited by Mohammed Ehsan Ullah Shareef

INTRODUCTION

Have you ever heard about Nikola Tesla? If your answer is no, you are missing out on a lot. But do not worry, in this article, you will learn enough to be impressed by his intelligence.

WHO IS NIKOLA TESLA?

Nikola Tesla is famous for his contributions to the alternative current (AC) electric supply system and engineering. However, Nikola Tesla fascinates people who believe in the power of the numbers and they will help them “manifest” their desires by his obsession to magnificence of numbers 3,6 and 9. In psychology, obsessive-compulsive disorder is sometimes defined as the disease of intelligent people losing their brilliance. Perhaps we can attribute Tesla’s intelligence to this, because he was right when he talked about electricity years before his contemporaries. What if the keys of the universe are hidden in the numbers of 3,6 and 9? What if he is right about this too?

THE MYSTIQUE OF NUMBERS

“As Nikola Tesla said, we are living in a place where numbers are real and alive. Numbers are not just representative symbols. The relationship between numbers is not random or man-made. But we will discover that they are actually the basics of everything that we will discover.” It has been attributed that Tesla said: “ If you only knew the magnificence of the numbers 3, 6 and 9, then you would have a key to the universe “. We cannot be sure if he actually said this phrase, but his obsession with numbers is a fact. What did they mean to him? Why are they so important? It has to do with complicated mathematics and human history. For example, when you repeatedly multiple 3 by 3 (3,9,27,81…) you will always end up with digits that sum up to 3 (3,9,9,18…). Similarly, when you multiply 6 by 3 (6, 18, 54, 162…), the digits also reduce to 3 or multiples of 3 (6, 9, 9, 9, etc.). For 9, the pattern is even more intriguing. Multiplying 9 by 3 (9, 27, 81, 243…) also results in digits that sum to 9 (9, 9, 9, 9, etc.). Also in Fibonacci Sequence, which is a famous mathematical pattern, where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones (e.g., 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and so on). Interestingly, when you create a pattern using the Fibonacci sequence and reduce the numbers to a single digit, it eventually repeats as 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 4, 3, 7, 1, 8, 9, 8, 8, and so on. Notice how 3, 6, and 9 are prominently present and form a repeating cycle. Threes also appear in human history. Triangles have three sides which are the same with pyramids. The “ Father, Son and Holy Ghost ” is just one of the examples of many trinities that have existed throughout human history. Tesla himself would emphasize the “ energy, frequency and vibration ” trifecta, which he thought held the “ secrets of the universe ”.

MAGNIFICENCE OF NUMBER IN HIS LİFE

In his life, he also used them in various fields. He would walk three times around the block before entering. Also washed his dishes with 18 napkins (18 is divisible by 3,6 and 9 ). He also would stay in hotel rooms that had a room number divisible by three as the room #3327 New Yorker Hotel in January 1943, where he died.

CONCLUSİON

Today Tesla’s mystical attachment to 3,6 and 9 has been used by millions around the world in social media platforms. Users are using the 3, 6, 9 method to manifest their ambitions and dreams into their lives. Actually it is easy to use this method, thanks to social media users, you only need to write what you want to manifest - it can be a new and healthy relationship or job, it is all about yourself - three times in morning, 6 times in afternoon and 9 times at night. As discussions, memes, and content circulate on these platforms, the mystique of these numbers endures, perpetuating interest and curiosity among a new generation. It may not be what Tesla actually talked about and obsessed over the 3,6 and 9. But the evidence that his ideas have not dimmed.
