Project to Control Surgical Errors

Posted by Raushan Pandit on December 20, 2022 Edited by Sandhya Viswanathan

Abstact

It is a project that controls surgical errors during surgery. It solves the problem by protecting from overdose of general anaesthesia and protects from loss of surgical equipment in the patient's body.

  1. Sometimes during surgery, a doctor may leave a piece of surgical equipment, e.g. sponges or instruments, inside a patient. We can prevent this from a tool box module. In this module, we can use a weight sensor that protects from loss of surgical equipment. During the end of surgery, this module checks whether all the equipment is placed on it or not. If all are placed then no equipment loss in the patient body takes place, if not then the module gives a signal as light or buzzer to check that and prevent loss.
  2. Among surgery errors, one of the problems is death from general anaesthesia.
  3. Also, infections occur due to purely unsterilized surgical equipment or tools during surgery. So, in the same module we may set up a water steam system and kill all the bacteria, germs, pathogens, viruses etc because hot water steam may kill that and sterilize the tools. Sometimes these tools weren't purely sterilized. So, infections occur due to this also. So, I think this module may be a solution to these errors.
  4. In addition, it also takes a step to control the performing of incision on the wrong body part or wrong location through a programmed machine (like the brain).

Introduction

A little more than 4,000 surgical errors occur each year (2020). Some surgical errors are as follows:

  • Injuring a nerve during surgery.
  • Infection.
  • Administering too much or too little medication, i.e. anaesthesia error.
  • Performing an incision at the wrong location.
  • Leaving a piece of surgical equipment, e.g. sponges or instruments, inside a patient.
  • Operating on the wrong body part.

Actuators and Sensors

  1. Weight sensor- It is a sensor that is used in that prototype or model for the safety or loss of surgical equipment.
  2. Nichrome- It is a material that is used for heating things in electrical devices. It is used in this module for the steam of water that controls the bacterial infection, viral infection through surgical equipment and tools.
  3. Arduino- Arduin is a microcontroller or CPU that controls the sensors. It is used to make this system automatic and easy. It also works as the brain that helps for the remembering and memorizing or assists surgeons to choose the right place for incision and right surgery. The data on this device checks the data and gives a result for the right place and right surgery. In this way it assists the surgeons like a reference book.
  4. External sensor display- It is used to check the results or data by this device or microcontroller. It is also used to type the data or record the data and compile into text and then match this data with programmed surgeries data and give right results as an assistant.

Proposed System and Architecture

Filter layer:- It is a layer that helps to wash the surgical tools with evaporated warm water. It helps to kill the bacteria and microbes.

Methodology

The working process of this instrument box module is simple. Firstly, it has an evaporating system that kills the bacteria and microbes and prevents patients from infection. It has weight sensors that prevent the patients from leaving the equipment in the surgical place by buzzer for warning and all programmed in microcontroller. We may add additional functions to prevent wrong place incision also.

Results and conclusion

It will be beneficial to control the surgical error during surgery like tools in organs etc. It also protects tools from being affected by bacteria, viruses or other pathogens. It has an instrument box with high efficiency in controlling the surgical error (approximately 70-75%).
