AI: The Future of Cyber Security?

Cybersecurity's Evolution: Navigating the Intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Digital Defense.

Posted by Saad Amin on August 21, 2023

Introduction

Due to increased cyber attacks, there has been an increase in demand and requirement for more effective and complex cyber security controls. Artificial Intelligence has been a great source in dealing with these cyber attacks. Cyber security has expanded in response to the need to protect the information and data currently available, and AI has been observed to significantly impact cyber security on a broad scale. Because of this, machine learning has been firmly incorporated into modern cyber security-related technology. This article provides a literature review on the impact of Ai in cyber security as well as telling its limitations in addition to suggesting further research to extend applications of AI and ML in this field.

Types of Cyber Threats

A sort of harmful software known as malware allows any file or program to be used against a computer user. Worms, viruses, Trojans, and spyware are a few examples of different kinds of malware.

Another form of malware known as ransom ware entails the attacker encrypting and locking the victim's computer system files before demanding money to unlock and decode them.

Attacks that rely on interpersonal contact are known as social engineering. To obtain sensitive information that is generally safeguarded, it tricks users into circumventing security measures.

Phishing is a type of social engineering in which phony emails or texts are delivered that appear to be from reliable or well-known sources. The purpose of these communications, which are frequently random attacks, is to collect personal information, including credit cards.

Limitations of AI in Cyber Security

There is always a chance for false positives in the application of AI in cyber security. Ai models are extremely sensitive to changes in conditions which can lead to false results and reduce trust in these systems. Furthermore, AI systems can be hacked which can lead to major security breaches for instance in the case of businesses.

Effects of AI on Cyber Security

AI results in both positive as well as negative effects. Area of technology has advanced massively and multiple industries have enhanced due to cyber security like Perol [2]. With the advent of Tech, malicious attacks have become more advanced causing more disruption since attackers have found more and more limits in cyber security systems. Due to ML systems, new algorithms are in place thus attackers can’t use old techniques to exploit systems. Adding AI and ML systems also showcases that mistakes are avoided. AI has been used in numerous ways for their part in cyber [3]

The future has been validated to boost AI on a wide scale due to which Ai will be further enhanced to incorporate it into the defense systems of companies. The majority of organizations have felt improvements using AI as well as reducing crimes like cyber malicious attacks. AI technology has greater detection capabilities than a human[4]. Moreover, AI has excellent security protection from hackers and cyber crimes. Real-time traffic monitoring enables AI technology to find activity without correct measurements and focus on actions. Conclusion

Artificial intelligence is a great asset in countering and mitigating cyber threats but there are still potential issues such as false positives and hacking which reduce its validity. Nonetheless, with the advent of technology, AI systems will eventually be the future of cyber security defending from hackers in addition to malicious threats. The rise of new technological learning in the learning of computers would substantially enhance cyber abilities. With the help of AI, a safer digital environment can be created.
